Frequently Asked Questions

How long can I keep the dumpster?

We offer flexible rental durations. Standard rentals cover up to 7 days, but extensions are available. We include scheduling upfront so you know pickup timing.

Are there items I cannot place in the dumpster?

Yes,hazardous materials, certain liquids, large pressurized tanks, and regulated items must be excluded. We provide a full list and recall help.

Do I need a permit to place the dumpster on the street or curb?

Possibly city or municipal rules may apply. We will assist you by providing container dimensions and guidance when a permit is required.

Can I overload the container?

No. Containers are rated for weight limits. Overloading can incur extra fees and may prevent pickup. We review materials and approximate weight with you beforehand.

What if my project runs longer than planned?

We accommodate extension of the rental period. You simply contact our team and we adjust scheduling—potentially with additional pickup if needed.

Can I mix construction debris and yard waste in the same bin?

Sometimes but it depends on load type and disposal destination. We evaluate the mix to ensure compliance and avoid higher disposal fees.
